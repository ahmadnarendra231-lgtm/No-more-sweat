Get to Know Our Product

No More Sweat (NMS) is a lightweight liquid primer specially formulated to control excess sweat and oil effectively. It helps your makeup stay flawless, long-lasting, and transfer-resistant throughout the day.

Designed to create a smooth and breathable base, No More Sweat keeps your skin looking fresh and matte without feeling heavy or sticky. Perfect for daily wear or special occasions, even in hot and humid conditions.
